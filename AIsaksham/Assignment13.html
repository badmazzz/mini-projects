Rann of Kutch

Experience the vast white salt desert of the Rann of Kutch during the Rann Utsav. Marvel at the surreal landscape, enjoy traditional folk music and dance, and indulge in the rich handicrafts of the region.

For more information, visit Gujarat Tourism - Rann of Kutch.

Dwarka

Visit the sacred city of Dwarka, home to the Dwarkadhish Temple. Explore the spiritual ambiance, take a dip in the holy Gomti River, and witness the vibrant aarti at the Dwarkadhish Temple.

For more information, visit Gujarat Tourism - Dwarka.

Gir National Park

Discover the diverse wildlife at Gir National Park, the last refuge of the Asiatic lions. Embark on a safari to spot lions, leopards, and various bird species amidst the picturesque landscapes.

For more information, visit Gujarat Tourism - Gir National Park.
